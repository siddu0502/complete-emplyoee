Break Policy

Lunch break is limited to 45 minutes, and short breaks are limited to 15 minutes each. Employees are expected to return to work within these allowed time limits. If the break duration exceeds the permitted time, the extra time will be added to the employee’s working hours, and their workday will be extended accordingly. Repeated violations may be subject to review by management.
